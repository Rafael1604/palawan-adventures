Puerto Princesa is known for its beautiful beaches and stunning landscapes. It is the capital city of the Province of Palawan and serves as the center of trade, commerce, communication, education, and government service. The city divides Palawan into northern and southern parts, covering about 17% of the province’s total land mass.

Puerto Princesa is known for its stunning landscapes, rich biodiversity, and vibrant marine life. It is home to the world-renowned Underground River, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The city is also a gateway to island-hopping adventures, eco-tourism, and cultural heritage sites.
